Hero Guides – In-depth Builds, Combos, and Counters

By Gian • March 15, 2025

Gusion

Gusion is one of the most versatile assassins in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. With his high burst damage and mobility, he can dominate the battlefield when played correctly.

Best Build for Gusion

  • Magic Shoes: Provides cooldown reduction for more frequent skill usage.
  • Clock of Destiny: Increases magic power and mana, scaling over time.
  • Lightning Truncheon: Adds burst damage and mana regeneration.
  • Concentrated Energy: Provides spell vamp for sustainability.
  • Holy Crystal: Boosts magic power significantly.
  • Blood Wings: Increases magic power and provides a shield.

Gusion Combos

  • Basic Combo: Skill 1 → Skill 2 → Basic Attack → Skill 1 (Return) → Skill 2 (Return).
  • Advanced Combo: Skill 1 → Skill 2 → Ultimate → Skill 2 → Skill 1 (Return) → Skill 2 (Return).

Counters for Gusion

  • Akai: Akai's crowd control can disrupt Gusion's combos.
  • Chou: Chou's immunity and crowd control make him a tough opponent.

Lancelot

Lancelot is a highly mobile assassin who excels at diving into fights and eliminating squishy targets. His skill set allows him to deal massive damage while dodging enemy attacks.

Best Build for Lancelot

  • Warrior Boots: Provides physical defense and movement speed.
  • Blade of Despair: Increases physical attack and damage against low-health enemies.
  • Endless Battle: Adds true damage and sustain.
  • Queen's Wings: Provides damage reduction and cooldown reduction.
  • Malefic Roar: Increases physical penetration.
  • Immortality: Grants a second chance in team fights.

Lancelot Combos

  • Basic Combo: Skill 1 → Skill 2 → Basic Attack → Skill 1 (Second Cast).
  • Advanced Combo: Skill 1 → Ultimate → Skill 2 → Skill 1 (Second Cast).

Counters for Lancelot

  • Khufra: Khufra's crowd control can stop Lancelot's dashes.
  • Franco: Franco's hook can catch Lancelot out of position.

Fanny

Fanny is one of the most mechanically challenging heroes in Mobile Legends. Her ability to zip around the map with her cables makes her a deadly assassin.

Best Build for Fanny

  • Warrior Boots: Provides physical defense and movement speed.
  • Bloodlust Axe: Adds spell vamp and cooldown reduction.
  • Blade of Despair: Increases physical attack and damage against low-health enemies.
  • Endless Battle: Adds true damage and sustain.
  • Queen's Wings: Provides damage reduction and cooldown reduction.
  • Immortality: Grants a second chance in team fights.

Fanny Combos

  • Basic Combo: Skill 2 (Cable) → Skill 1 → Skill 2 (Cable) → Skill 1.
  • Advanced Combo: Skill 2 (Cable) → Ultimate → Skill 1 → Skill 2 (Cable).

Counters for Fanny

  • Khufra: Khufra's crowd control can stop Fanny's cables.
  • Lolita: Lolita's shield can block Fanny's damage.
